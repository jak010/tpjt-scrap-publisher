What is Scrap Publisher?


If new information or updates occur within your group, you can create and subscribe to an RSS feed for them. By using an RSS feed, you can easily and quickly check for any updates, and this can help facilitate communication and collaboration within the group.

Additionally, by using a service that connects RSS feeds with email, you can receive RSS feeds via email. By setting it up this way, you can immediately receive updates via email and stay on top of your work or project progress in real-time.
